<h1> Bombing Prevention Awareness Course (BPAC)</center></h1>

<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>The Bombing Prevention Awareness Course (BPAC) provides emergency responders with an overview of bombing-prevention measures. During the one-day course, responders learn to understand basic concepts of general bombing prevention knowledge.</p>
<p>Below are some, but not all, of the critical skill sets learned during this training program.</p>

<ul>
    <li>Evaluate the function and purpose of improvised explosive devices (IEDs).</li>
    <li>Identify explosive effects of IEDs.</li>
    <li>Evaluate the terrorist attack style.</li>
	<li>Identify indicators of suspicious behaviors and items.</li>
	<li>Identify protective measures.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Course Length</h2>
<p>1.0 days</p>
